Community Train Alerts

Get instant notifications when a train is approaching the crossing.

Click "Allow" when your browser asks for permission.

Android users: The notification prompt will appear automatically — just tap "Allow".

iPhone/iPad users: For notifications to work reliably, tap the Share button (square with arrow) at the bottom → choose "Add to Home Screen". Then open the app from your Home Screen and allow notifications when prompted.

You'll receive alerts like "Train approaching" and "Train has passed".
